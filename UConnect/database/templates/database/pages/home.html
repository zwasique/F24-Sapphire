Welcome to UConnect!



A source for students to connect with each other, specifically a way for them to build projects together. They will make postings that other students can see.
These postings will be projects other students from different majors can interact with if they are interested. Students can then work together to get experience, while learning the ropes!

The need for this work is obvious, here are some ways students can benefit:

Students need experience to get experience now, companies that hire "entry level jobs" are now
asking these new hires to have one or two years of experience. This causes trouble for many college students looking for ways to get into the work force.
Our solution is to provide a way for students to connect with each other and gain experience while still learning.
